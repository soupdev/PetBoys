About us

Your favorite family wagger deserves the care of a qaulified and friendly dog walker. Our team of dedicated pet care professionals are local, certified in pet safety, bonded and insured, and well trained in the service of pet care from walks, wags, and everything in between. Founded in 2010, PetBoys is comprised of a team of animal lovers who specialize in dog care but also offer quality love and care to cats, birds, hamsters, gerbils, mice, snakes, lizards, fish, and even your houseplants! All of our walkers pass a criminal background check and recieve a training course specialized in the unique customer care your furry family is looking for. Want to meet us? We can't wait to meet you!

Services

Walks

Every wag deserves a walk! Our professional team of local dog-walkers will take your wagger off to explore the neighborhood, scout squirrels, and do some bird watching. Have a high energy pup? Ask about our extended power walks!

Overnight Stays

We can keep your doggo well exercised, fed, entertained and cuddled up in the comfort of their own home while you're away. Overnight stays include several daily walks and a bath before you return so your buddy is looking thier best!

Training

Good manners are important for any pup if they want lots of friends! Our certified trainers can help your doggo master the basics or impress the dog-park with a multitude of tricks. We also specialize in helping enforce behavior you set at home.

Adopt A Wag Program

The PetBoys 'Adopt A Wag' program aims to help loveable scallywags find forever homes. We'd love to meet your adorable foster or adoptee and help them get well exercised and acclimated with thier neighborhood. Since our founding in 2010 we've helped over 200 dogs find families that love and care for them like the honorable furry family members they are. If you are looking for your next adventure and a loyal companion to go with it, you can't go wrong with fostering or adopting a sweet shelter dog who could use your love and care. We've partnered up with Pet Finder to make it easy to find dogs in your neighborhood who can't wait to meet you! Every wag deserves a walk but mnore importantly, every dog deserves a home.
