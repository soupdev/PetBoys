Adopt a Wag

The PetBoys 'Adopt A Wag' program aims to help loveable scallywags find forever homes. If you are looking for your next adventure and a loyal companion to go with it, We've partnered up with Pet Finder to make it easy to find dogs in your neighborhood who can't wait to meet you! Every wag deserves a walk but mnore importantly, every dog deserves a home.

    Every adopted pet recieves:

  • Dog Walking Kit with leash, collar,
    and roll of sanitry bags with leash clip
  • 7 Free Walks
  • 7 Free Training Sessions
